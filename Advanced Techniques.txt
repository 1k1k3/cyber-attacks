
### 3. **Add Python Scripts**:
   Add Python scripts for each type of cyber attack simulation. Here are some categories you can include:

   - **Brute Force Attacks**: Tools that simulate brute-forcing passwords for various services like SSH or HTTP.
   - **Denial of Service (DoS) Attacks**: Scripts that simulate flooding servers with traffic.
   - **Phishing Simulations**: Email or web-based phishing simulation tools.
   - **Network Attacks**: Tools for ARP spoofing, DNS poisoning, or other network attacks.
   - **Advanced Techniques**: Reverse shells, privilege escalation scripts, etc.
